# Privacy Policy for Shortcodes

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Shortcodes ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles data.

## 1. No Data Collection
Shortcodes **does not collect, transmit, or store** any of your personal information, browsing history, or the text you type on any external servers. We have no way of accessing the data you create or use within the extension.

## 2. Local Storage
All your custom aliases, emoji overrides, and preferences are stored **locally** on your device using Chrome's `storage.local` API. This data never leaves your browser and is only used to provide the extension's core functionality (expanding your shortcuts).

## 3. Permissions Usage
*   **`storage`**: Used solely to save your custom shortcuts and settings on your computer.
*   **Host Permissions (`<all_urls>`)**: Used only to inject the content script that detects the ":" trigger and provides the autocomplete dropdown in text fields. The extension does not track or record your activity on these sites.

## 4. No Third-Party Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, we do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to third parties.
